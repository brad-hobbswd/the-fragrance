A Story of Surrender

A shattered vessel. A room filled with fragrance. A single act of worship heaven refused to forget.

Inside a quiet loft above city streets, Elena arrives carrying years of disappointment, grief, and silent pain. Resting within her hands sits a treasured alabaster perfume purchased for a future that never arrived.

Others see waste. Joshua sees beauty.

The Fragrance invites readers into a journey of surrender, healing, worship, and devotion poured out before Jesus.

Heaven remembers moments that human eyes often overlook.
